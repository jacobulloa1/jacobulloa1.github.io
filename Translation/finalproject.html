Nightmare in Astroworld

This is my submission for my digital media art class in 2021. The overall story I wanted to tell here is
what happened during the festival compared to what people know in the news. To try and provide a new persepctive on the tragedy that
occurred on that night.

Click on the images to enlarge them.

This first GIF I made was to illistrate the perspective of someone in the crowd at the height of the crowd surge during the festival.

My second GIF I produced is a perspective someone in the crowd as the show progresses and the growing and overwhelming force of people almost consuming them just as many experienced.

This was the last GIF I was able to make. I decided to make this because I wanted to draw a the contrast between the experience of Kylie Jenner versus many concert goers that night. She was criticized for posting on her Instagram story during the horrid event and kept them up even after the news broke of many people getting injured and some losing their lives.
