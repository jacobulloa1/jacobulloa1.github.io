Meme Mashup

I elected to combine Pepe the Frog and Pedro Pascal Crying. The meaning behind this
is to represent how the original artist of Pepe has felt towards many online bigoted right wing communities
have essentially appropriated the meme for themeselves. Something the artist originally intended to be
innocent but has turned into a symbol associated with hate.
