My Glitch project

Here are my submissions for the Glitch project in Art 74. The goal was to alter pictures
in a way that conveyed some sort of message. I picked three images I felt were representative
of American Democracy. I decided to alter these images because I wanted to represent how fundamentally
broken our practice of democracy is here in the United States.
